5. Cookieless Analytics & Behavioural Tracking: In addition to consent-based Google Analytics, AI Sentia uses two privacy-preserving, cookieless analytics systems that operate without requiring your consent under GDPR, UK GDPR, and CCPA:

  • Microsoft Clarity (Cookieless Mode): We use Microsoft Clarity with cookieless mode enabled. Clarity collects anonymised behavioural data including heatmaps (aggregated cursor positions and click patterns) and session replays. In cookieless mode, no persistent identifiers or cookies are set. Data is processed in aggregate and cannot be used to identify individual visitors. Please refer to Microsoft's Privacy Statement for full details.
  • Cookieless Event Analytics: We collect anonymous, aggregated interaction counts — including page visits, scroll depth milestones (25%, 50%, 75%, 100%), persona filter selections, timeframe changes, and article link clicks. No cookies, device fingerprints, IP addresses, or personal identifiers are stored. Only aggregate counters (e.g. "50% scroll happened 42 times on 2026-03-04") are retained and automatically deleted after 90 days. This processing is conducted on the basis of our legitimate interest in understanding how our free service is used, and does not require consent as no personal data is processed.

You may opt out of Microsoft Clarity data collection at any time by visiting clarity.microsoft.com/optout . The cookieless event counters collect no personal data and therefore no opt-out mechanism is required or applicable.

6. Your Rights (UK & EEA Visitors): If you are located in the United Kingdom or European Economic Area, you have rights under data protection law, including the right to withdraw consent to analytics tracking at any time by rejecting cookies or clearing your browser storage.
